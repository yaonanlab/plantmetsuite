standards database:Constructed from standards and contains information on m/z, MS/MS and retention times.

Public databases:MoNA (http://mona.fiehnlab.ucdavis.edu), MassBank(Horai et al., 2010), HMDB (Wishart et al., 2022), KEGG(Kanehisa et al., 2004), Respect (Sawada et al., 2012) and CASMI(Oberacher, 2013).

MS/MS spectral tag database(MS2T):Collected by LC-MS contains information on identified and unidentified metabolic profiles of Arabidopsis cauline leaves, rosette leaves, stems and inflorescence parts, which is useful for studying tissue specificity and novel functions of secondary metabolites in plants(Matsuda et al., 2009).
