Free forever No registration required 

1. The parameters are divided into two categories: required and optional. Required fields are mandatory, and optional fields are modified as needed.

2.The uploaded data needs to be one of the requirements.

3.Two download links are provided. The first button, "View data format", is a piece of text data that can be viewed by clicking on it and saved by right-clicking on Save As. The second button, "Get sample data", downloads a zip archive with sample data in other formats that we have prepared.

4. The results are provided in three formats to choose from: png, pdf, svg, download as needed.

5. After submission, the system will provide you with a series of task numbers. With this task number, we will save it for you free of charge for 24 h. After 24 h, it will be deleted automatically. Please keep it as you need.
